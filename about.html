Who We Are

More Than an IT Company

Founded over a decade ago, Harinit started as a small managed services provider with a handful of clients and a big ambition — to redefine what IT support could look like.

Today, we serve 150+ organisations across industries including finance, healthcare, retail, and manufacturing. Our team of certified engineers brings decades of combined expertise to every engagement.

We're not just here to fix tickets. We're here to be a strategic technology partner that helps your business grow, adapt, and thrive in an increasingly digital world.
